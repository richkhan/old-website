About Me

I am an Entreprenuer.

I am CEO of two start up companies. Lurik Labs and Lurik Academy. I was inspired to start Lurik Labs with a couple of my friends after my research internship. My topic was Using VR to Improve Learning about Ecosystem. During the research process I realized how the combination of education with VR can assist in learning many topics from the STEM fields. I decided to use what I learned from my first research,(A Framework for Measuring Creativity in Collaborative Digital Spaces) about design based research and game based learning to be integrated into a VR environment. The goal is to create a fully simulated world in VR. Since I like gaming I want to teach game development and as a result promote game based learning. Hence the second company Lurik Academy.

I am a Software Engineer

When I entered college I was a Biology major with the aim going to medical school. However, in the back of my mind I wanted to program, create and build systems, and of course create simulated worlds. So after some thought I switch over to Computer Science, at first it was a challenge, however I began to enjoy the many different aspects of computer science. From low level assembly language, to high level C++, Java, and Python. I enjoyed learning how things worked and figuring out solution to difficult problems. When learning Java I enjoyed how straightforward it was to pick up. I learned how to learn a new programming language in my Java journey. My goal is to become graphics programmer.
